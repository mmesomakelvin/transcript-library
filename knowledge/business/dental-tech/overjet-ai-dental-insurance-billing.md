# AI for Dental Insurance Billing: Why Overjet Leads the Way

_Published: July 1, 2025 by Mario Paganini, Senior VP of Marketing, Overjet_

## Executive Summary

Dental insurance billing is transforming through artificial intelligence (AI), with Overjet leading the industry as the first FDA-cleared dental AI platform. This comprehensive guide explores how AI addresses traditional billing challenges and why Overjet's solution stands out for dental professionals seeking improved efficiency, reduced claim denials, and faster revenue cycles.

## Key Challenges in Traditional Dental Billing

### Manual Process Pain Points

- **Claim Denials**: Typos, missing information, and incorrect codes lead to rejected claims
- **Time-Consuming Reviews**: Staff spend hours reviewing patient charts to justify claims
- **Inconsistent Documentation**: Different providers document and code in varying ways
- **Financial Strain**: Delayed payments impact cash flow and increase labor costs

### Impact on Practices

- Increased risk of lost revenue from denials
- Limited time for patient care due to administrative burden
- Higher compliance and audit risks
- Hindered growth and profitability

## How AI Transforms Dental Billing

### Core AI Capabilities

1. **Automation**
   - Reduces manual chart reviews and data entry
   - Extracts and organizes clinical information instantly
2. **Accuracy**
   - Ensures correct procedure codes and documentation
   - Minimizes errors leading to claim denials
3. **Speed**
   - Analyzes data in real-time
   - Prepares claims faster than manual workflows

### Advanced Features

- **Real-Time Analysis**: AI reads dental X-rays and clinical notes immediately upon capture
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies documentation patterns required by insurance companies
- **Predictive Analytics**: Predicts claim approval rates to prioritize submissions
