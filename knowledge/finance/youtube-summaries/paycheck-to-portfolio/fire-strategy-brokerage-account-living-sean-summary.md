## Detailed Analysis

### Core FIRE Strategy Implementation

Sean's approach centers on treating his brokerage account as both investment vehicle and checking account. He deposits his entire W2 income directly into E\*TRADE and immediately invests 100% into dividend-producing assets. Rather than maintaining cash reserves, he uses margin borrowing to cover living expenses.

**Key Philosophy**: "Buy assets, live off those assets, and really never sell those assets unless it's specific situations where you know a specific asset's going to go up in value and you know a specific asset's going to come down in value."

### Specific Holdings and Investment Strategy

Sean's portfolio includes:

- **SPY**: S&P 500 ETF with DRIP enabled
- **McDonald's (MCD)**: Individual stock with "healthy dividend"
- **CLM and CRF**: Closed-end funds that DRIP at NAV (Net Asset Value)
- **Cornerstone**: Currently undergoing rights offering (causing temporary volatility)

### Risk Management Through Margin

Sean maintains at least 50% margin equity within his account, comparing this to a home equity line of credit. Example analogy: "If your house's worth $500,000 and you owe $250,000 on it, the bank will allow you to pull a certain amount of money out on that at a discounted rate because it's secured with your asset."
