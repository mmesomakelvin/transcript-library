### Return of Capital (ROC) Deep Dive

A critical misunderstanding Sean addresses is that ROC equals "portfolio decay" or "losing money." This is incorrect.

**What ROC Actually Is:**
- Tax classification, not economic reality
- Reduces cost basis instead of being taxed today
- Defers taxes until you sell the position
- Common in covered call ETFs and CLM funds

**Evidence Provided:**
- QQQI: 99% ROC classification for September payment
- YieldMax funds: 51-75% ROC classifications
- Morning Star data: QQQI outperforms QQQ when dividends are reinvested

**Tax Benefit Example:**
- $40,424 in dividends received
- 75% classified as ROC = $30,318 not taxed today
- 25% classified as ordinary = $10,106 taxable
- At 15% tax rate = $1,516 in taxes
- Effective rate on total income: 3.75%
